TimelyTextView
==============

Animated TextView like Timely app

Intital commit for Animated TextView present in the Timely(Alarm) App.
This is just an addition to the concept explained by Sriram Ramani here : http://sriramramani.wordpress.com/2013/10/14/number-tweening/

I have just figured out missing bits & pieces and made a simple library out of it. Please thank Sriram if this helped you. Also please note I have been very busy and this was the work of 3hrs on a lazy Monday afternoon, so there might be a few bugs and lots of scope for improvement
